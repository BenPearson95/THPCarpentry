Garage Conversion

Project information

  • Category: Building, Projects
  • Location: Crowborough
  • Project date: July 2015 - September 2015

We turned the existing double garage into a lovely living space within the property. The conversion included constructing a new bay window to the front with aesthetics in keeping with the existing building. Internally we installed a new insulated sub floor, a wall to wall cabinet and changed the internal door lay-out to better-suit the ground floor.
