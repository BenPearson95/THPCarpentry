Wardrobes

Project information

  • Category: Joinery & Carpentry, Projects
  • Location: Crowborough
  • Project date: December 2016

Two opposing wardrobes built in situ to suit the sloped ceiling pitch degree. Wardrobes featured a great balance of draw space and hanging space to best suit the clients preferences.

Wardrobes constructed using birch ply carcassing with Tulipwood facework providing the ideal surface for a quality paint finish.
