    The Services We Offer

    Carpentry Framing

    All structual and non-structual timber framing elements covered.

    Extensions

    All home extensions covered. Both masonry and timber frame designs.

    Loft Conversions

    Covering all elements from start to finish. Stripping out to restructuring & rennovating

    Second Fix Carpentry

    Covering everything from internal doors, skirting, architrave, window boards and more.

    Cabinetry

    Covering everything from fitted wardrobes, bookshelves, TV units, alcove shelving, larder units and other bespoke furniture.

    Kitchens

    Supply & Fit service for your new kitchen. We can provide a bespoke fitted kitchen to your design preference, and also a fitting service for pre-fabricated kitchens.

    Joinery

    Supply & Fit service utilising our own workshop space. We cover bespoke timber casement windows, box sash windows, doors, etc...

    Painting

    We do provide a painting service for all of the work that we do. Ranging from a sprayed factory finished for our woodwork, to hand-painted internal finishes to walls & woodwork.
